Our Story

Dedicated to Missouri's Election Integrity

Show-Me Election Solutions was established to fill a critical need in Missouri's election infrastructure — expert, accessible consulting tailored specifically to the realities Missouri county election authorities face every day.

Our team brings direct experience working within Missouri's election ecosystem. We understand the operational pressures of running elections in counties of every size, from the largest metropolitan jurisdictions to the smallest rural offices. That firsthand knowledge informs every recommendation we make and every training session we deliver.

We believe that well-prepared election officials are the cornerstone of democratic integrity. Our mission is to equip Missouri's election authorities with the knowledge, tools, and documentation they need to conduct elections that are lawful, efficient, transparent, and accessible to every eligible voter.

Core Expertise

Areas of Deep Knowledge

Our consulting is grounded in specialized expertise across the key systems, laws, and standards that govern Missouri elections.

Missouri Election Law

Comprehensive understanding of Missouri Revised Statutes Chapter 115 and related election code provisions. We translate complex legal requirements into clear, actionable guidance for election office staff at every level.

MCVR System Knowledge

Extensive hands-on experience with the Missouri Centralized Voter Registration system. From voter record management and list maintenance to reporting and data integrity, we help offices maximize their effectiveness within MCVR.

HAVA Compliance

Deep familiarity with Help America Vote Act requirements and their practical implementation in Missouri. We guide election authorities through compliance auditing, provisional ballot procedures, voter identification standards, and accessibility mandates.

Election Security

Working knowledge of election security frameworks from CISA and the EAC. We help counties implement practical security measures for voting systems, election networks, physical ballot security, and chain-of-custody protocols.

Tabulation & Canvassing

Experience with the tabulation systems and canvassing procedures used across Missouri. We provide guidance on logic and accuracy testing, results reconciliation, post-election auditing, and certification processes.

Accessibility & ADA

Practical knowledge of ADA requirements for polling locations and election materials. We conduct accessibility assessments and develop plans to ensure every voter can participate fully and independently in the electoral process.
