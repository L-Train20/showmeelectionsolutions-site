Our Services

Election Administration
Expertise You Can Count On

Comprehensive consulting services designed specifically for Missouri county election authorities and their unique operational needs.

Service Area

Training & Certification

Our training programs are built from the ground up for Missouri election administration. We prepare election officials, deputy clerks, and poll workers with the knowledge and confidence they need to execute elections flawlessly — from pre-election setup through canvassing and certification.

  • Election judge and poll worker training programs customized for Missouri statutes
  • Continuing education sessions for county clerks and election directors
  • MCVR (Missouri Centralized Voter Registration) system training and best practices
  • Election-day procedures, provisional ballot handling, and ADA compliance training
  • Tabulation equipment certification and logic & accuracy testing procedures
Service Area

Compliance Consulting

Navigating the complex landscape of federal and state election law requires specialized knowledge. Our compliance consulting ensures your office meets every requirement — from HAVA mandates to Missouri Revised Statutes Chapter 115 — reducing risk and protecting the integrity of your elections.

  • Help America Vote Act (HAVA) compliance auditing and remediation
  • Missouri election law interpretation and practical application guidance
  • Election security assessments aligned with CISA and EAC standards
  • Voter registration compliance and NVRA list maintenance procedures
  • ADA accessibility compliance for polling locations and election materials
Service Area

Policy & Document Services

Well-crafted election policies and procedures are the backbone of a professional, defensible election operation. We develop, review, and refine the documentation your office needs — built on current Missouri law and best practices from election authorities across the state.

  • Comprehensive election procedures manuals tailored to your county
  • Emergency and contingency planning documentation for election operations
  • Canvassing and post-election audit procedures and checklists
  • Records retention schedules compliant with Missouri Secretary of State guidelines
  • Ballot design review and proofing processes per state requirements
