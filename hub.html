§115.124.3 — No-Election Authority Tracker
RSMo §115.124.3 — Municipalities ≤2,000 population may submit to voters the question of skipping elections when candidates = seats and no ballot measures are present. If adopted, authority covers that calendar year plus six calendar years following. At expiration, municipality loses the authority and must re-adopt by ballot. Missing the renewal window forces a full contested election.
